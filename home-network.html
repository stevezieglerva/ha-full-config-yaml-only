📶 Ziegler Home Network

Physical topology — AT&T fiber + Google Wifi mesh

Ethernet (wired) Wireless backhaul AT&T equipment — SSID asprings-att Google Wifi node — SSID asprings Wall drop / endpoint
Path summary: Internet → AT&T fiber router (closet, gateway & DHCP, broadcasts asprings-att). The router feeds four wired drops: Google Wifi #1 (closet, mesh primary) plus the Living room, Office, and Playroom drops. Google Wifi #1 backhauls over structured cabling to the Master room drop, which feeds Google Wifi #2 (master). Google Wifi #3 joins the mesh over wireless backhaul. The Google mesh broadcasts asprings.
Two WiFi networks coexist: asprings-att straight off the AT&T router and asprings off the Google mesh. Worth confirming which one your devices (HomePods, HA box, Matter hub) actually sit on — Matter/mDNS wants everything on the same subnet/VLAN.
