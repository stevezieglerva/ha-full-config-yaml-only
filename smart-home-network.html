How it reads: Home Assistant is the brain. Devices reach it over three transports — the Zigbee2MQTT mesh (now saturated, which is why the M3 was added), the Matter stack (native Matter gear plus the Aqara Hub M3 bridging re-homed Aqara sensors), and cloud/LAN integrations. Apple Home and Alexa are peer ecosystems that share state with HA through input_boolean virtual switches; Alexa also directly owns the ceiling-fan lights HA can't see.
